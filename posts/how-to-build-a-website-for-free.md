Building a website used to require hiring a developer and spending hundreds of dollars. Today, you can build a professional-looking website for free in under an hour. This guide walks you through everything step by step.

## What You Will Need

- A computer or smartphone
- A free email address
- 30–60 minutes of your time

No coding experience is required.

## Step 1 — Choose Your Website Builder

There are many free website builders available today. The best ones for beginners are:

| Builder | Best For | Free Plan |
|---|---|---|
| **Netlify** | Developers and bloggers | ✅ Yes |
| **Wix** | Small businesses | ✅ Yes |
| **Framer** | Portfolio and landing pages | ✅ Yes |
| **Google Sites** | Simple internal pages | ✅ Yes |

For this tutorial we will use **Netlify** because it is fast, reliable, and scales well as your site grows.

## Step 2 — Create a Free Netlify Account

1. Go to [netlify.com](https://netlify.com)
2. Click **Sign up** in the top right corner
3. Sign up with your GitHub or Google account — this is the fastest option
4. Once signed in you will land on your Netlify dashboard

## Step 3 — Create Your First Site

Netlify lets you drag and drop a folder to deploy a site instantly.

1. On your dashboard click **Add new site**
2. Select **Deploy manually**
3. Create a simple folder on your computer called `my-website`
4. Inside it create a file called `index.html` and paste this:

```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
  <head>
    <title>My First Website</title>
  </head>
  <body>
    <h1>Welcome to My Website</h1>
    <p>This is my first website — built for free!</p>
  </body>
</html>
```

5. Drag the `my-website` folder into the Netlify deploy area
6. Your site is live in seconds at a URL like `random-name.netlify.app`

## Step 4 — Customize Your Site

Now that your site is live you can start customizing it. Edit your `index.html` file to:

- Change the title to your name or business name
- Add your contact information
- Include links to your social media profiles

## Step 5 — Connect a Custom Domain (Optional)

Your free Netlify URL works fine to start, but a custom domain like `yourbusiness.com` looks more professional.

1. Buy a domain from [Cloudflare Registrar](https://cloudflare.com) for around $10/year
2. In Netlify go to **Site settings → Domain management**
3. Click **Add custom domain** and enter your domain
4. Follow the DNS instructions Netlify provides

Your site will be live on your custom domain within minutes.

## Summary

You have now built and deployed a real website for free. Here is what you accomplished:

- Created a Netlify account
- Deployed your first site
- Learned how to connect a custom domain

## What to Learn Next

Now that your site is live, here are some good next steps:

- Learn basic HTML and CSS to customize your design
- Add a contact form using [Netlify Forms](https://netlify.com/products/forms)
- Set up Google Analytics to track your visitors
